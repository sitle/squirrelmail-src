Needed APIs

sm_mail    - Retrieving mail (IMAP, NNTP, POP)
sm_prefs   - Get/Set preferences (filesystem, MySQL)
sm_abook   - Handle address books (filesystem, MySQL)
sm_logs    - Logging (filesystem, E-mail, MySQL)
sm_send    - Sends email out (SMTP, Sendmail)
sm_auth    - Handle authentication (IMAP, yp, MySQL)
sm_session - Session handling (PHP, filesystem, MySQL)
